## Product and Pricing Notes

Northstar Metrics sells three plans:

- **Starter** — €49/month
- **Growth** — €199/month
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing

Customers on the Starter and Growth plans are billed monthly by default.

Enterprise customers may be billed monthly or annually depending on contract terms.

## Security and Access

For employee access to internal systems:

- All employees must use SSO with multi-factor authentication.
- New hires receive access through the onboarding team.
- Access reviews are conducted every quarter.
- Production access is restricted to approved engineering staff.

## Onboarding Notes

The onboarding team is led by **Nina Petrova**.

During onboarding, new customers receive:

1. Workspace setup
2. SSO configuration help
3. Import assistance for historical CSV data
4. A kickoff call with a customer success manager

Typical onboarding duration for Growth customers is **7 to 10 business days**.
