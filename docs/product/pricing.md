# Pricing and Plans

## Starter
Starter customers are billed monthly by default.
Starter includes email support during standard support hours.

## Growth
Growth customers are billed monthly by default.
Growth includes priority weekday support and onboarding guidance.

## Enterprise
Enterprise contracts may be billed monthly or annually depending on the signed agreement.
Enterprise customers receive named onboarding contacts and access to escalation channels.
