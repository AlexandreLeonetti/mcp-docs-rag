# Product Overview

Northstar Metrics offers three main product surfaces:
- Northstar Core: operational analytics dashboards
- Northstar Assist: AI-assisted support summarization and triage
- Northstar Flow: workflow automation and escalations

Northstar Assist is the newest product line and is heavily used by support teams for summarizing tickets, drafting replies, and highlighting account risk.
