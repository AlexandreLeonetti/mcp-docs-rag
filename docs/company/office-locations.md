# Office Locations

Northstar Metrics operates from three main locations:
- Paris: headquarters, executive leadership, product strategy
- Lyon: customer support and operations hub
- Barcelona: sales and partnerships office

Paris is considered the official headquarters.
Barcelona is the main commercial office.
