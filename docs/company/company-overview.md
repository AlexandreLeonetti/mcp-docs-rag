# Northstar Metrics

Northstar Metrics is a fictional B2B SaaS company providing analytics, workflow automation, and support tooling for logistics teams, e-commerce operators, and internal operations departments.

## Mission
Northstar Metrics helps distributed operations teams detect issues faster, standardize responses, and reduce operational delays.
