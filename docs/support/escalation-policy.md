# Escalation Policy

Support agents escalate issues when:
- production access is blocked for approved staff
- billing failures affect multiple customers
- authentication incidents affect more than one tenant
- a VIP customer reports repeated outages within 7 days

Escalations should be opened within 30 minutes for Sev-1 incidents and within 2 hours for Sev-2 incidents.
