# Incident — Login Outage, November 2025

## Summary
Between 08:42 and 10:16 CET, some customers experienced login failures caused by an SSO redirect loop.

## Impact
Affected tenants could not complete login.
Support received repeated reports from Growth and Enterprise accounts.

## Immediate Response
Engineering disabled the faulty redirect rule and support posted status updates to affected customers.
